Frequently Asked Questions

What is the total cost of drilling a borehole?

The cost of drilling a borehole depends on the location and depth of the borehole. Kindly get in touch with us for a quotation.

What is a hydro-geological report?

Once the geologist has surveyed the area, he/she will provide you with a detailed report on the geology of the parcel of land. This report will also indicate the estimated depth of the water and the exact location of the borehole.

Why do I need water analysis report?

After drilling the borehole and doing test pumping, we will take a water sample to the government laboratory for testing. The test results in a water analysis report indicates whether or not the water is safe for domestic/industrial consumption.

Want to Know about DaySpring?

Dayspring Geoservices is a leading fast growing borehole drilling contractors in Nigeria. It was established in 2014 by a geologist, a graduate of University Ibadan, Nigeria and with a vision to improve water borehole industry. All the equipment used in the execution of projects at Dayspring are modern, which include Rotary Drilling Rigs and Elgi Compressors. These clearly put us ahead of the competition in the drilling industry. Drilling method adopted is either Down The Hole (DTH) drilling method or mud drilling, depending on the terrain to be drilled (basement complex or sedimentary).
Dayspring Geoservices has performed drilling works in southwestern region of Nigeria for approximately 3 years. With this experience, we have extensive knowledge in the field of borehole drilling and understand requirements of specific customers as well as projects. The company has been involved in a number of projects involving groundwater exploration, exploitation and evaluation, pumping test, pump recommendation and installation, water test, solid mineral exploration etc.
Currently the company employs two geologists, two drillers and two assistants with over 10 years combined experience in drilling and maintenance of water boreholes and the machines. The Company has a total of nine employees, who form part of the drilling crew in their respective units.
At Dayspring, we have the capacity and expertise needed to satisfy all your drilling requirements. The company is fast growing and is establishing an exceptional reputation as a provider of quality service with international recognition. All our works meet local and international standards.
